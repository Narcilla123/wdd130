INFOWATCH

Website for new Overwatch 2 Players

Overwatch 2 is a vibrant, fast-paced, team-based first-person shooter that combines exciting gameplay with deep strategy and a colorful cast of unique heroes. Set in a futuristic world where heroes and villains collide, players choose from a roster of characters, each with distinct abilities and roles. The game emphasizes teamwork, where players must coordinate to achieve objectives and outsmart the opposing team.

TANK

In Overwatch 2, a tank plays a crucial role as the team's frontline protector and space creator. Tanks are designed to absorb damage, control areas of the map, and protect their teammates. Each tank has unique abilities that allow them to shield allies, disrupt enemy positioning, or initiate engagements.

Damage (DPS)

In Overwatch 2, the Damage (DPS) role focuses on dealing high amounts of damage to enemies and securing eliminations. DPS heroes are versatile and designed to target key opponents, break down defenses, and create opportunities for their team to succeed.

Support

In Overwatch 2, the Support role is essential for keeping the team alive and ensuring they can perform at their best. Support heroes provide healing, buffs, and other utilities to sustain teammates and counter enemy threats. While their primary focus is aiding allies, support heroes are also capable of dealing damage and defending themselves when needed.
